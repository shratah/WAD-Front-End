Support Center

Welcome to the Celestial Home Support Center. Here you can find answers to your questions and get help with any issues you might encounter.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1: How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Follow the instructions to complete your purchase.

Q2: What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other online payment options.

Q3: How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your order on our website.

Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues, please check the solutions below:

  • Issue 1: Cannot log in to your account - Ensure you are using the correct email and password. Reset your password if necessary.
  • Issue 2: Payment declined - Check your payment details and ensure you have sufficient funds. Try a different payment method if the issue persists.
  • Issue 3: Product not as described - Contact our support team with your order number and details of the issue.
